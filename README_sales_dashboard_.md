# Data Sales Dashboard w/ Power BI


## Introduction

This dashboard was created for **sales insight, top seling products and to analyze the sales by year/month ** to give a full insight for products and their companies: information about the profit/loss for the sales data. Using a real-world dataset of contoso sales data. (including category, unit cost/ unit quantity and locations), this project provides a single, easy-to-use interface to explore market trends and compensation.

## Skills Showcased

This project was a journey through key Power BI features. Here's a look at what i learned :

-   **⚙️ Data Transformation (ETL) with Power Query:** Cleaned, shaped, and prepared the raw data for analysis by handling blanks, changing data types, and creating new columns.
-   **🧮 Implicit Measures:** Formulated measures to derive key insights and KPIs like `total_revenue` .
-   **📊 Core Charts:** Utilized **Column, Bar, Line,**  to compare total_products_sold and track trends over time.
-   **🗺️ Geospatial Analysis:** Leveraged **tree map** to visualize the global distribution of orders.
-   **🔢 KPI Indicators & Tables:** Used **Cards** to display key metrics and **Tables** to provide granular, sortable data.
-   **🎨 Dashboard Design:** Designed an intuitive and visually appealing layout, exploring both common and uncommon chart types to best tell the data story.
-   **🖱️ Interactive Reporting:**
    -   **Slicers:** To dynamically filter the report by product category.
    -   **Buttons :** To create a seamless navigation experience.
    -   **Drill-Through:** To navigate from a high-level summary to a contextual, detailed view.

## Dashboard Overview

*This report is split into two distinct pages to provide a high-level summary and a detailed analysis.*


### Page 1: Contoso sales insight

This page showcases the total insight for every prdouct , with total profit in KPI card , it shows the median cost price by median revenue by product and shows the trendline for the total units sold by the time period.

### Page 2 : sales deep dive

This is the deep-dive page. From the main dashboard, you can drill through to this view to get specific details for a product, including gender ranges, , top selling companies, and a tree map of product delivered locations.
